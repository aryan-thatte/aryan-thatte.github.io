McHacks7 — 3rd Place

I built an IoT device on Raspberry Pi that aids the visually impaired by informing them of their environment real time. It uses ultrasonic sensors that causes a repetitve sound to play within the user's headphones that increases its rate as it gets closer to an object. This inturn enables the user to navigate themselves safely and securely.
